# Twilight Imperium 4 - Agendas Reference

## Agendas (63 Total)

1. **Archived Secret** (Directive) - The elected player draws 1 secret objective.

2. **Arms Reduction** (Directive) - Each player destroys all but 2 of his dreadnoughts and all but 4 of his cruisers.

3. **Colonial Redistribution** (Directive) - Destroy each unit on the elected planet. Then, the player who controls that planet chooses 1 player with the fewest victory points; that player may place 1 infantry from his reinforcements on the elected planet.

4. **Compensated Disarmament** (Directive) - Destroy each ground force on the elected planet; for each unit that was destroyed, the player who control that planet gains 1 trade good.

5. **Economic Equality** (Directive) - Each player returns all of his trade goods to the supply. Then, each player gains 5 trade goods.

6. **Incentive Program** (Directive) - Draw and reveal 1 stage I public objective from the deck and place it near the public objectives.

7. **Ixthian Artifact** (Directive) - The speaker rolls 1 die. If the result is 6-10, each player may research 2 technologies. If the result is 1-5, destroy all units in Mecatol Rex's system, and each player with units in systems adjacent to Mecatol Rex's system destroys 3 of his units in each of those systems.

8. **Judicial Abolishment** (Directive) - Discard the elected law from play.

9. **Miscount Disclosed** (Directive) - Vote on the elected law as if it were just revealed from the top of the deck.

10. **Mutiny** (Directive) - Each player that voted "For" gains 1 victory point.

11. **New Constitution** (Directive) - Discard all laws from play. At the start of the next strategy phase, each player exhausts each planet in his home system.

12. **Public Execution** (Directive) - The elected player discards all of his action cards. If he has the speaker token, he gives it to the player on his left. The elected player cannot vote on any agendas during this agenda phase.

13. **Seed of an Empire** (Directive) - The player with the most victory points gains 1 victory point.

14. **Swords to Plowshares** (Directive) - Each player destroys half of his infantry on each planet he controls, rounded up. Then, each player gains trade goods equal to the number of his infantry that were destroyed.

15. **Unconventional Measures** (Directive) - Each player that voted "For" draws 2 action cards.

16. **Wormhole Research** (Directive) - Each player who has 1 or more ships in a system that contains a wormhole may research 1 technology. Then, destroy all ships in systems that contain an alpha or beta wormhole.

17. **Anti-Intelectual Revolution** (Law) - After a player researches a technology, he must destroy 1 of his non-fighter ships.

18. **Classified Document Leaks** (Law) - The elected secret objective becomes a public objective; place it near the other public objectives in the common play area.

19. **Comittee Formation** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. Before players vote on an agenda that requires a player to be elected, the owner of this card may discard this card to choose a player to be elected. Players do not vote on that agenda.

20. **Conventions of War** (Law) - Players cannot use BOMBARDMENT against units that are on cultural planets.

21. **Core Mining  (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. Then, destroy 1 infantry on the planet. The resource value of this planet is increased by 2.

22. **Demilitarized Zone  (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. Then, destroy all units on that planet. Player's units cannot land, be produced, or be placed on this planet.

23. **Enforced Travel Ban** (Law) - Alpha and beta wormholes have no effect during movement.

24. **Executive Sanctions** (Law) - Each player can have a maximum of 3 action cards in his hand.

25. **Fleet Regulations** (Law) - Each player cannot have more than 4 tokens in his fleet pool.

26. **Holy Planet of Ixth (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. The planet's owner gains 1 victory point. Units on this planet cannot use PRODUCTION. When a player gains control of this planet, he gains 1 victory point. When a player loses control of this planet, he loses 1 victory point.

27. **Homeland Defense Act** (Law) - Each player can have any number of PDS units on planets he controls.

28. **Imperial Arbiter** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. At the end of the strategy phase, the owner of this card may discard this card to swap 1 of his strategy cards with 1 of another player's strategy cards.

29. **Minister of Commerce** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. After the owner of this card replenishes commodities, he gains 1 trade good for each player that is his neighbor.

30. **Minister of Exploration** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. When the owner of this card gains control of a planet, he gains 1 trade good.

31. **Minister of Industry** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. When the owner of this card places a space dock in a system, his units in that system may use their PRODUCTION abilities.

32. **Minister of Peace** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. After a player activates a system that contains 1 or more of a different player's units, the owner of this card may discard this card; immediately end the active player's turn.

33. **Minister of Policy** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. At the end of the status phase, the owner of this card draws 1 action card.

34. **Minister of Sciences** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. When the owner of this card resolves the primary or secondary ability of the "Technology" strategy card, he does not need to spend resources to research technology.

35. **Minister of War** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. The owner of this card may discard this card after performing an action to remove 1 of his command counters from the game board and return it to his reinforcements; then he may perform 1 additional action.

36. **Prophecy of Ixth** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. The owner of this card applies +1 to the result of his fighter's combat rolls. When the owner of this card uses PRODUCTION, he discards this card unless he produces 2 or more fighters.

37. **Publicize Weapon Schematics** (Law) - If any player owns a war sun technology, all player may ignore all prerequisites on war sun technologies. All war suns lose Sustain Damage.

38. **Regulated Conscription** (Law) - When a player produces units, he produces only 1 fighter and infantry for its cost instead of 2.

39. **Representatative Government (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Player cannot exhaust planets to cast votes during the agenda phase; each player may cast 1 vote on each agenda instead.

40. **Research Team: Biotic (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. When the owner of this planet researches technology, he may exhaust this card to ignore 1 green prerequisite.

41. **Research Team: Cybernetic (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. When the owner of this planet researches technology, he may exhaust this card to ignore 1 yellow prerequisite.

42. **Research Team: Propulsion (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. When the owner of this planet researches technology, he may exhaust this card to ignore 1 blue prerequisite.

43. **Research Team: Warfare (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. When the owner of this planet researches technology, he may exhaust this card to ignore 1 red prerequisite.

44. **Senate Sanctuary (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. The influence value of this planet is increased by 2.

45. **Shard of the Throne  (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - The elected player gains this card and 1 victory point. A player gains this card and 1 victory point when he wins a combat against the owner of this card. Then, the previous owner of this card loses 1 victory point.

46. **Shared Research** (Law) - Each player's units can move through nebulae.

47. **Terraforming Initiative (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - Attach this card to the elected planet's card. The resource and influence values of this planet are increased by 1.

48. **The Crown of Emphidia (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - The elected player gains this card and 1 victory point. A player gains this card and 1 victory point after he gains control of a planet in the home system of this card's owner. Then, the previous owner of this card loses 1 victory point.

49. **The Crown of Thanlos (Removed with PoK)** (Law) - The elected player gains this card. During each combat round, the owner of this card may reroll any number of dice; he must destroy each of his units that did not produce a hit with its reroll.

50. **Wormhole Reconstruction** (Law) - All systems that contain either an alpha or beta wormhole are adjacent to each other.

51. **Armed Forces Standardization** (Directive) - The elected player place command tokens from their reinforcements so that they have 3 tokens in their tactic pool, 3 tokens in their fleet pool, and 2 tokens in their strategy pool. They return any excess tokens to their reinforcements.

52. **Articles of War** (Law) - All mechs lose their printed abilities except for Sustain Damage.

53. **Checks and Balances** (Law) - When a player chooses a strategy card during the strategy phase, they give that strategy card to another player who does not have 1 (or a player who does not have 2 is a three- or four-player game), if able.

54. **Clandestine Operations** (Directive) - Each player removes 2 command tokens from their command sheet and return those tokens to their reinforcements.

56. **Galactic Crisis Pact** (Directive) - Each player may perform the secondary ability of the elected strategy card without spending a command token; command tokens placed by the ability are placed from a player's reinforcements instead.

57. **Minister of Antiquities** (Directive) - The elected player gains 1 relic.

58. **Nexus Sovereignty** (Law) - Alpha and beta wormholes in the wormhole nexus have no effect during movement.

59. **Political Censure** (Law) - The elected player gains this card and 1 victory point. The elected player cannot play action cards. If the owner of this card loses this card, they lose 1 victory point.

60. **Rearmament Agreement** (Directive) - Each player places 1 mech from their reinforcements on a planet they control in their home system.

61. **Representative Government** (Law) - Players cannot exhaust planets to cast votes during the agenda phase; each player may cast 1 vote on each agenda instead. Players cannot cast additional votes.

62. **Research Grant Reallocation** (Directive) - The elected player gains any 1 technology of their choice. Then, for each prerequisite on that technology, they remove 1 token from their fleet pool and return it to their reinforcements.

63. **Search Warrant** (Law) - The elected player gains this card and draws 2 secret objectives. The owner of this card plays with their secret objectives revealed.
