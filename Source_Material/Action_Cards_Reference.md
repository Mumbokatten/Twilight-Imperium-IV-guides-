# Twilight Imperium 4 - Action Cards Reference

## Action Cards (106 Total)

1. **Ancient Burial Sites** - At the start of the agenda phase:: Choose 1 player. Exhaust each cultural planet owned by that player.

2. **Assassinate Representative** - After an agenda is revealed:: Choose 1 player. That player cannot vote on this agenda.

3. **Bribery** - After the speaker votes on an agenda:: Spend any number of trade goods. For each trade good spent, cast 1 additional vote for any outcome.

4. **Bunker** - At the start of an invasion:: During this invasion, apply -4 to the result of each BOMBARDMENT roll against planets you control.

5. **Confusing Legal Text** - When you are elected as the outcome of an agenda:: Choose 1 player. That player is the elected player instead.

6. **Construction Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, place 1 space dock from your reinforcements on a planet you control.

7. **Courageous to the End** - After 1 of your ships is destroyed during a space combat:: Roll 2 dice. For each result equal to or greater than that ship's combat value, your opponent must choose and destroy 1 of his ships.

8. **Cripple Defenses** - ACTION: Choose 1 planet.: Destroy each PDS on that planet.

9. **Diplomacy Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, choose 1 system that contains a planet you control. Each other player places a command token from their reinforcements in that system.

10. **Direct Hit** - After another player's ship uses Sustain Damage to cancel a hit produced by your units:: Destroy that ship.

11. **Disable** - At the start of an invasion in a system that contains 1 or more of your opponents' PDS units:: Your opponents' PDS units lose Planetary Shield and SPACE CANNON during this invasion.

12. **Distinguished Councilor** - After you cast votes on an outcome of an agenda:: Cast 5 additional votes for that outcome.

13. **Economic Initiative** - ACTION:: Ready each cultural planet you control.

14. **Emergency Repairs** - At the start or end of a combat round: Repair all of your units that have Sustain Damage in the active system.

15. **Experimental Battlestation** - After another player moves ships into a system during a tactical ACTION:: Choose 1 of your space docks that is either in or adjacent to that system. That space dock uses SPACE CANNON 5 (x3) against ships in the active system.

16. **Fighter Prototype** - At the start of the first round of a space combat:: Apply +2 to the result of  each of your fighters' combat rolls during this combat round.

17. **Fire Team** - After your ground forces make combat rolls during a round of ground combat:: Reroll any number of your dice.

18. **Flank Speed** - After you activate a system:: Apply +1 to the move value of each of your ships during this tactical action.

19. **Focused Research** - ACTION:: Spend 4 trade goods to research 1 technology

20. **Frontline Deployment** - ACTION:: Place 3 infantry from your reinforcements on 1 planet you control.

21. **Ghost Ship** - ACTION:: Place 1 destroyer from your reinforcements in a non-home system that contains a wormhole and does not contain other players' ships.

22. **Imperial Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, gain 1 victory point.

23. **In The Silence Of Space** - After you activate a system:: Choose 1 system. During this tactical action, your ships in the chosen system can move through systems that contain other players' ships.

24. **Industrial Initiative** - ACTION:: Gain 1 trade good for each industrial planet you control.

25. **Infiltrate** - When you gain control of a planet:: Replace each PDS and space dock that is on that planet with a matching unit from your reinforcements.

26. **Insubordination** - ACTION:: Remove 1 token from another player's tactic pool and return it to his reinforcements.

27. **Intercept** - After your opponent declares a retreat during a space combat:: Your opponent cannot retreat during this round of space combat.

28. **Leadership Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, gain 3 command tokens.

29. **Lost Star Chart** - After you activate a system:: During this tactical action, systems that contain alpha and beta wormholes are adjacent to each other.

30. **Lucky Shot** - ACTION:: Destroy 1 dreadnought, cruiser, or destroyer in a system that contains a planet you control.

31. **Maneuvering Jets** - Before you assign hits produced by another player's SPACE CANNON roll:: Cancel 1 hit.

32. **Mining Initiative** - ACTION:: Gain trade goods equal to the resource value of 1 planet you control.

33. **Morale Boost** - At the start of a combat round:: Apply +1 to the result of each of your unit's combat rolls during this combat round.

34. **Parley** - After another player commits units to land on a planet you control:: Return the committed units to the space area.

35. **Plague** - ACTION: Choose 1 planet that is controlled by another player.: Roll 1 die for each infantry on that planet. For each result of 6 or greater, destroy 1 of those units.

36. **Political Stability** - When you would return your strategy card(s) during the status phase:: Do not return your strategy card(s). You do not choose strategy cards during the next strategy phase.

37. **Politics Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, draw 3 action cards and gain the speaker token.

38. **Public Disgrace** - When another player chooses a strategy card during the strategy phase:: That player must choose a different strategy card instead, if able.

39. **Reactor Meltdown** - ACTION:: Destroy 1 space dock in a non-home system.

40. **Reparations** - After another player gains control of a planet you control:: Exhaust 1 planet that player controls and ready 1 planet you control.

41. **Repeal Law** - ACTION:: Discard 1 law from play.

42. **Rise of a Messiah** - ACTION:: Place 1 infantry from your reinforcements on each planet you control.

43. **Sabotage** - When another player plays an action card other than "Sabotage":: Cancel that action card.

44. **Salvage** - After you win a space combat:: Your opponent gives you all of his commodities.

45. **Shields Holding** - Before you assign hits to your ships during a space combat:: Cancel up to 2 hits.

46. **Signal Jamming** - ACTION:: Chose 1 non-home system that contains or is adjacent to 1 of your ships. Place a command token from another player's reinforcements in that system.

47. **Skilled Retreat** - At the start of a combat round:: Move all of your ships from the active system into an adjacent system that does not contain another player's ships; the space combat ends in a draw. Then, place a command token from your reinforcements in that system.

48. **Spy** - ACTION:: Choose 1 player. That player gives you 1 random action card from his hand.

49. **Summit** - At the start of the strategy phase:: Gain 2 command tokens.

50. **Tactical BOMBARDMENT** - ACTION:: Choose 1 system that contains 1 or more of your units that have BOMBARDMENT. Exhaust each planet controlled by other players in that system.

51. **Technology Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, research 1 technology.

52. **Trade Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, gain 5 trade goods.

53. **Unexpected Action** - ACTION:: Remove 1 of your command tokens from the game board and return it to your reinforcements.

54. **Unstable Planet** - ACTION:: Choose 1 harzadrous planet. Exhaust that planet and destroy up to 3 infantry on it.

55. **Upgrade** - After you activate a system that contains 1 or more of your ships:: Replace 1 of your cruisers in that system with 1 dreadnought from your reinforcements.

56. **Uprising** - ACTION:: Exhaust 1 non-home planet controlled by another player. Then gain trade goods equal to its resource value.

57. **Veto** - When an agenda is revealed:: Discard that agenda and reveal 1 agenda from the top of the deck. players vote on this agenda instead.

58. **War Effort** - ACTION:: Place 1 cruiser from your reinforcements in a system that contains 1 or more of your ships.

59. **Warfare Rider** - After an agenda is revealed:: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, place 1 dreadnought from your reinforcements in a system that contains 1 or more of your ships.

60. **Blitz** - At the start of an invasion: Each of your non-fighter ships in the active system that do not have BOMBARDMENT gain BOMBARDMENT 6 until the end of the invasion.

61. **Counterstroke** - After a player activates a system that contains 1 of your command tokens: Return that command token to your tactic pool.

62. **Fighter Conscription** - Action: Place 1 fighter from your reinforcements in each system that contains 1 or more of your space docks or units that have capacity; they cannot be placed in systems that contain other players' ships.

63. **Forward Supply Base** - After another player activates a system that contains your units: Gain 3 trade goods. Then, choose another player to gain 1 trade good.

64. **Ghost Squad** - After a player commits units to land on a planet you control: Move any number of ground forces from any planet you control in the active system to any other planet you control in the active system.

65. **Hack Election** - After an agenda is revealed: During this agenda, voting begins with the player to the right of the speaker and continues counterclockwise.

66. **Harness Energy** - After you activate an anomaly: Replenish your commodities.

67. **Impersonation** - Action: Spend 3 influence to draw 1 secret objective.

68. **Insider Information** - After an agenda is revealed: Look at the top 3 Cards of the agenda deck.

69. **Master Plan** - After you perform an action: You may perform an additional action this turn.

70. **Plagiarize** - Action: Spend 5 influence and choose a non-faction technology owned by 1 of your neighbors; gain that technology.

71. **Rally** - After you activate a system that contains another player's ships: Place 2 command tokens from your reinforcements in your fleet pool.

72. **Reflective Shielding** - When one of your ships uses Sustain Damage during combat: Produce 2 hits against your opponent's ships in the active system.

73. **Sanction** - After an agenda is revealed: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, each player that voted for that outcome returns 1 command token from their fleet supply to their reinforcements.

74. **Scramble Frequency** - After another player makes a BOMBARDMENT, SPACE CANNON, or ANTI-FIGHTER BARRAGE roll:: That player rerolls all of their dice.

75. **Solar Flare** - After you activate a system: During this movement, other players cannot use SPACE CANNON against your ships.

76. **War Machine** - When 1 or more of your units use PRODUCTION: Apply +4 to the total PRODUCTION value of your units and reduce the combined cost of the produced units by 1.

77. **Archaeological Expedition** - Action: Reveal the top 3 cards of an exploration deck that matches a planet you control; gain any relic fragments that you reveal and discard the rest.

78. **Confounding Legal Text** - When another player is elected as the outcome of an agenda: You are the elected player instead.

79. **Coup D'etat** - When another player would perform a strategic ACTION:: End that player's turn; the strategic action is not resolved and the strategy card is not exhausted.

80. **Deadly Plot** - During the agenda phase when an outcome would be resolved: If you voted for or predicted another outcome, discard the agenda instead; the agenda is resolved with no effect and it is not replaced. Then, exhaust all of your planets.

81. **Decoy Operation** - After another player activates a system that contains 1 or more of your structures: Remove up to 2 of your ground forces from the game board and place them on a planet you control in the active system.

82. **Diplomatic Pressure** - When an agenda is revealed: Choose another player; that player must give you 1 promissory note from their hand.

83. **Divert Funding** - Action: Return a non-unit upgrade, non-faction technology that you own to your technology deck. Then, research another technology.

84. **Exploration Probe** - Action: Explore a frontier token that is in or adjacent to a system that contains 1 or more of your ships.

85. **Manipulate Investments** - At the start of the strategy phase:: Place a total of 5 trade goods from the supply on strategy cards of your choice; you must place these tokens on at least 3 different cards.

86. **Nav Suite** - After you activate a system: During the "Movement" step of this tactical action, ignore the effect of anomalies.

87. **Refit Troops** - Action: Choose 1 or 2 of your infantry on the game board; replace each of those infantry with mechs.

88. **Reveal Prototype** - At the start of a combat: Spend 4 resources to research a unit upgrade technology of the same type as 1 of your units that is participating in this combat.

89. **Reverse Engineer** - After another player discards an action card that has a component ACTION:: Take that action card from the discard pile.

90. **Rout** - At the start of the "Announce Retreats" step of space combat, if you are the defender: Your opponent must announce a retreat, if able.

91. **Scuttle** - Action: Choose 1 or 2 of your non-fighter ships on the game board and return them to your reinforcements; gain trade goods equal to the combined cost of those ships.

92. **Seize Artifact** - Action: Choose 1 of your neighbors that has 1 or more relic fragments. That player must give you 1 relic fragment of your choice.

93. **Waylay** - Before you roll dice for ANTI-FIGHTER BARRAGE: Hits from this roll are produced against all ships (not just fighters).

94. **Black Market Dealings** - : When you are negotiating a transACTION: You and the other player can include relics, action cards, and unscored secret objectives as part of the transaction. This card cannot be canceled.

95. **Brilliance** - : ACTION: Ready 1 of your planets that has a technology specialty or choose 1 player to gain their breakthrough.

96. **Crash Landing** - : When your last ship in the active system is destroyed: Place 1 of your ground forces from the space area of the active system onto a planet in that system other than Mecatol Rex; if the planet contains other players' units, place your ground force into coexistence.

97. **Crisis** - : At the end of any player's turn, if there are at least 2 players who have not passed: Skip the next player's turn.

98. **Exchange Program** - : ACTION: Choose another player. You and that player may agree to place 1 infantry from each of you reinforcements into coexistence on a planet the other player controls that contains their ground forces; if no agreement is reached, you each discard 1 token from your fleet pool.

99. **Extreme Duress** - : At the start of another player's turn, if they have a readied strategy card: If that player's next action is not a strategic action, they discard all of their action cards, gives you all of their trade goods, and shows you all of their secret objectives.

100. **Lie in Wait** - : After 2 of your neighbours resolve a transACTION: Look at each of those player's hands of action cards, then choose and take 1 action card from each.

101. **Mercenary Contract** - : ACTION: Spend 2 trade goods to place 2 neutral infantry onany non-home planet that contains no units; if that planet was owned by another player, they return its planet card to the planet card deck.

102. **Pirate Contract** - : ACTION: Place 1 neutral destroyer in a non-home system that contains no non-neutral ships.

103. **Pirate Fleet** - : ACTION: Spend 3 resources to place 1 neutral carrier, 1 neutral cruiser, 1 neutral destroyer, and 2 neutral fighters in a non-home system that contains no ships.

104. **Rescue** - : After another player moves ships into a system that contains your ships: You may move 1 of your ships into the active system from any system that does not contain one of your command tokens.

105. **Strategize** - : ACTION: Perform the secondary ability of any readied or unchosen strategy card

106. **Overrule** - : ACTION: Perform the primary ability of any readied or unchosen strategy card
