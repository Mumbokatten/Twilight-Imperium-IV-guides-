# Comprehensive Alliance Commanders Reference

This document lists all alliance commanders sorted by frequency of appearance in faction guides (Arborec, Argent Flight, Barony of Letnev, Mentak Coalition).

## Ranking Methodology

Commanders are ranked by total appearances across all 4 guides:
- **Top Tier** = Super Top Tier or Top Tier appearance
- **Strong Tier** = Really Useful or Useful appearance

---

## Complete Ranking (All 30 Factions - Ranked by Appearance Frequency)

### Tier 1: Most Frequently Recommended (4 appearances each)

**1. Titans of Ul - Tungstantus**
- Unlock: Have 5 structures on the game board
- Ability: When 1 or more of your units use PRODUCTION, you may gain 1 trade good
- Appearances: Arborec (Super Top), Argent (Super Top), Mentak (Top), Barony (Really Useful)

**2. Nomad - Navarch Feng**
- Unlock: Have 1 scored secret objective
- Ability: You can produce your flagship without spending resources
- Appearances: Arborec (Super Top), Argent (Super Top), Barony (Super Top), Mentak (Useful)

**3. Embers of Muaat - Magmus**
- Unlock: Produce a war sun
- Ability: After you spend a token from your strategy pool, you may gain 1 trade good
- Appearances: Barony (Super Top), Mentak (Top), Arborec (Really Useful), Argent (Really Useful)

### Tier 2: Very Strong (3 appearances)

**4. Winnu - Rickar Rickani**
- Unlock: Control Mecatol Rex or enter into a combat in the Mecatol Rex system
- Ability: During combat, apply +2 to the result of each of your unit's combat rolls in the Mecatol Rex system, your home system, and each system that contains a legendary planet
- Appearances: Arborec (Super Top), Argent (Super Top), Barony (Super Top)

### Tier 3: Strong (2 appearances)

**5. Universities of Jol-Nar - Ta Zern**
- Unlock: Own 8 technologies
- Ability: After you roll dice for a unit ability, you may reroll any of those dice
- Appearances: Argent (Super Top), Barony (Super Top)

**6. Deepwrought Scholarate - Aello**
- Unlock: Have an ocean card in play
- Ability: When another player spends resources to research a technology, that player may reduce the cost by 1; if they do, gain 1 commodity or convert 1 of your commodities to a trade good
- Appearances: Mentak (Top), Arborec (Really Useful)

**7. Naaz-Rokha Alliance - Dart and Tai**
- Unlock: Have mechs in 3 systems
- Ability: After you gain control of a planet that was controlled by another player, you may explore that planet
- Appearances: Arborec (Really Useful), Barony (Really Useful)

### Tier 4: Useful (1 appearance)

**8. L1Z1X Mindnet - 2RAM**
- Unlock: Have 4 dreadnoughts on the game board
- Ability: Units that have Planetary Shield do not prevent you from using BOMBARDMENT
- Appearances: Barony (Super Top)

**9. Sardakk N'orr - G'hom Sek'kus**
- Unlock: Control 5 planets in non-home systems
- Ability: During the "Commit Ground Forces" step, you can commit up to 1 ground force from each planet in the active system and each planet in adjacent systems that do not contain 1 of your command tokens
- Appearances: Arborec (Super Top)

**10. Crimson Rebellion - Ahk Siever**
- Unlock: Place a breach token in a system that contains another player's unit
- Ability: At the end of a combat between any players, gain 1 commodity or convert 1 of your commodities to a trade good
- Appearances: Mentak (Top)

**11. Xxcha Kingdom - Elder Qanoj**
- Unlock: Control planets that have a combined total of at least 12 influence
- Ability: Each planet you exhaust to cast votes provides 1 additional vote. Game effects cannot prevent you from voting on an agenda
- Appearances: Argent (Really Useful)

**12. Yin Brotherhood - Brother Omar**
- Unlock: Use one of your faction abilities
- Ability: This card satisfies a green technology prerequisite. When you research a tech owned by another player, you may return 1 of your infantry to reinforcements to ignore its prerequisites
- Appearances: Argent (Really Useful), Mentak (Useful)

**13. Empyrean - Xuange**
- Unlock: Be neighbors with all other players
- Ability: After another player moves ships into a system that contains 1 of your command tokens, you may return that token to your reinforcements
- Appearances: Argent (Really Useful), Mentak (Useful)

**14. Clan of Saar - Rowl Sarring**

- Appearances: Arborec (Really Useful)

**15. Nekro Virus - Nekro Acidos**

- Appearances: Barony (Really Useful)

**16. Mentak Coalition - S'ula Mentarion**

- Appearances: Barony (Really Useful)

## Deepwrought Scholarate Specific Recommendations

Based on Deepwrought's unique kit (coexistence spread, tech acquisition via breakthrough, strong economy):

**Top Tier:**
1. **Nekro Virus (Nekro Acidos)** - Turn constant tech acquisition into action card generation
2. **Nomad (Navarch Feng)** - Produce flagship without spending resources. Saves 8 resources, always good value

**Strong Tier:**
3. **Vuil'raith Cabal (That Which Molds Flesh)** - Extra infantry production for coexistence network
4. **Xxcha (Elder Qanoj)** - Works with ocean cards that provide influence. Strong political multiplier
5. **Yssaril (So Ata)** - Information gathering from widespread coexistence. Strong deterrent to remove coexisting units and ocean cards
6. **Federation of Sol (Claire Gibson)** - Extra defensive infantry when your coexistence planets are invaded
7. **Winnu (Rickar Rickani)** - +2 combat in Mecatol Rex, home system, and legendary planet systems for key battles and home defense
8. **Empyrean (Xuange)** - Command token efficiency for sneaky invasion plays from coexisting positions
