# Twilight Imperium 4 - Objectives Reference

## Stage I Objectives (20 Total) - 1 VP Each

1. **Erect a Monument** - Spend 8 resources.
2. **Sway the Council** - Spend 8 influence.
3. **Negotiate Trade Routes** - Spend 5 trade goods.
4. **Lead from the Front** - Spend 3 tokens from tactic/strategy pools.
5. **Diversify Research** - Own 2 technologies in each of 2 colors.
6. **Develop Weaponry** - Own 2 unit upgrade technologies.
7. **Found Research Outposts** - Control 3 planets with technology specialties.
8. **Intimidate the Council** - Have ships in 2 systems adjacent to Mecatol Rex.
9. **Expand Borders** - Control 6 planets in non-home systems.
10. **Corner the Market** - Control 4 planets with same trait.
11. **Amass Wealth** - Spend 3 influence, 3 resources, 3 trade goods.
12. **Build Defenses** - Have 4 or more structures.
13. **Discover Lost Outposts** - Control 2 planets with attachments.
14. **Engineer a Marvel** - Have flagship or war sun on board.
15. **Explore Deep Space** - Have units in 3 systems without planets.
16. **Improve Infrastructure** - Have structures on 3 planets outside home system.
17. **Make History** - Have units in 2 systems with legendary/MR/anomalies.
18. **Populate the Outer Rim** - Have units in 3 edge systems (not home).
19. **Push Boundaries** - Control more planets than each neighbor.
20. **Raise a Fleet** - Have 5+ non-fighter ships in 1 system.

## Stage II Objectives (18 Total) - 2 VP Each

1. **Found a Golden Age** - Spend 16 resources.
2. **Manipulate Galactic Law** - Spend 16 influence.
3. **Centralize Galactic Trade** - Spend 10 trade goods.
4. **Galvanize the People** - Spend 6 tokens from tactic/strategy pools.
5. **Master of Sciences** - Own 2 technologies in each of 4 colors.
6. **Revolutionize Warfare** - Own 3 unit upgrade technologies.
7. **Conquer the Weak** - Control 1 planet in another player's home system.
8. **Subdue the Galaxy** - Control 11 planets in non-home systems.
9. **Unify the Colonies** - Control 6 planets with same trait.
10. **Hold Vast Reserves** - Spend 6 influence, 6 resources, 6 trade goods.
11. **Construct Massive Cities** - Have 7+ structures.
12. **Reclaim Ancient Monuments** - Control 3 planets with attachments.
13. **Achieve Supremacy** - Have flagship/war sun in opponent's home or MR.
14. **Patrol Vast Territories** - Have units in 5 systems without planets.
15. **Protect the Border** - Have structures on 5 planets outside home.
16. **Become a Legend** - Have units in 4 systems with legendary/MR/anomalies.
17. **Control the Borderlands** - Have units in 5 edge systems (not home).
18. **Rule Distant Lands** - Control 2 planets in/adjacent to different opponent homes.
19. **Command an Armada** - Have 8+ non-fighter ships in 1 system.

## Secret Objectives (40 Total) - 1 VP Each

1. **Unveil Flagship** - Win space combat with flagship (can't score if destroyed).
2. **Turn their Fleets to Dust** - SPACE CANNON destroy last ship in system.
3. **Destroy their Greatest Ship** - Destroy war sun or flagship.
4. **Spark a Rebellion** - Win combat vs VP leader.
5. **Threaten Enemies** - Ships adjacent to opponent's home.
6. **Make an Example** - BOMBARDMENT destroy last ground forces on planet.
7. **Cut Supply Lines** - Ships in system with enemy space dock.
8. **Monopolize Production** - Control 4 industrial planets.
9. **Mine Rare Minerals** - Control 4 hazardous planets.
10. **Forge an Alliance** - Control 4 cultural planets.
11. **Become a Martyr** - Lose control of planet in home system.
12. **Betray a Friend** - Win combat vs player whose PN you have.
13. **Brave the Void** - Win combat in anomaly.
14. **Darken the Skies** - Win combat in opponent's home.
15. **Defy Space and Time** - Units in wormhole nexus.
16. **Demonstrate your Power** - 3+ non-fighter ships after space combat.
17. **Destroy Heretical Works** - Purge 2 relic fragments.
18. **Dictate Policy** - 3+ laws in play.
19. **Drive the Debate** - You/your planet elected by agenda.
20. **Establish Hegemony** - Control planets with 12+ influence.
21. **Adapt New Strategies** - Own 2 faction technologies.
22. **Master the Laws of Physics** - Own 4 tech of same color.
23. **Form a Spy Network** - Discard 5 action cards.
24. **Gather a Mighty Fleet** - Have 5 dreadnoughts.
25. **Establish a Perimeter** - Have 4 PDS.
26. **Fuel the War Machine** - Have 3 space docks.
27. **Become the Gatekeeper** - Ships in alpha and beta wormholes.
28. **Learn Secrets of the Cosmos** - Ships in 3 systems adjacent to anomalies.
29. **Control the Region** - Ships in 6 systems.
30. **Occupy the Seat of the Empire** - Control MR with 3+ ships.
31. **Fight With Precision** - AFB destroy last fighter.
32. **Foster Cohesion** - Neighbors with all players.
33. **Hoard Raw Materials** - Control planets with 12+ resources.
34. **Mechanize the Military** - 1 mech on each of 4 planets.
35. **Occupy the Fringe** - 9+ ground forces on planet without space dock.
36. **Produce en Masse** - Units with 8+ PRODUCTION in single system.
37. **Prove Endurance** - Last to pass.
38. **Seize an Icon** - Control legendary planet.
39. **Stake Your Claim** - Control planet in contested system.
40. **Strengthen Bonds** - Have opponent's PN in play area.
