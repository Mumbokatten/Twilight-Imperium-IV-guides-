# Alliance Commander Ranking (1-31)

## Ranking Criteria (General)
- Universal value (works for most factions)
- Economy/resource generation
- Combat improvements
- Flexibility and utility
- Ease of unlock

---

## Complete Ranking

**1. Nomad (Navarch Feng)**
- Unlock: Have 1 scored secret objective
- Ability: Produce flagship without spending resources
- Why: Saves 8 resources. Always good value.

**2. Crimson Rebellion (Ahk Siever)**
- Unlock: Place breach token in system with another player's unit
- Ability: At end of combat, gain commodity/TG
- Why: Combat-focused faction. Addresses TG access weakness.

**3. Deepwrought (Aello)**
- Unlock: Have an ocean card in play
- Ability: When others research tech, gain commodity/TG if they take -1 discount
- Why: Passive TG income. Addresses TG access weakness.

**4. Muaat (Magmus)**
- Unlock: Produce a war sun
- Ability: After you spend strategy token, gain 1 TG
- Why: Passive TG income. Addresses TG access weakness.

**5. Winnu (Rickar Rickani)**
- Unlock: Control MR or enter combat in MR system
- Ability: Apply +2 combat in MR, home system, legendary planet systems
- Why: Strong combat boost in key battles.

**6. Titans of Ul (Tungstantus)**
- Unlock: Have 5 structures on game board
- Ability: Gain 1 TG when using PRODUCTION
- Why: Passive TG income every production.

**7. Empyrean (Xuange)**
- Unlock: Be neighbors with all other players
- Ability: After another player moves ships into system with your token, return token to reinforcements
- Why: Late game slaying tool.

**8. Vuil'raith Cabal (That Which Molds Flesh)**
- Unlock: Have units in 3 gravity rifts
- Ability: Up to 2 fighters/infantry don't count against PRODUCTION limit
- Why: Extra production capacity. Not free - still cost resources but excluded from production limit.

**9. Nekro Virus (Nekro Acidos)**
- Unlock: Own 3 technologies
- Ability: After you gain tech, draw 1 action card
- Why: Tech-heavy factions benefit.

**10. Barony of Letnev (Rear Admiral Farran)**
- Unlock: Have 5 non-fighter ships in 1 system
- Ability: After unit uses Sustain Damage, gain 1 TG
- Why: TG from dreadnoughts with sustain damage.

**11. Naaz-Rokha (Dart and Tai)**
- Unlock: Have mechs in 3 systems
- Ability: After you gain control of planet from another player, explore that planet
- Why: Extra value from conquests.

**12. Mentak Coalition (S'ula Mentarion)**
- Unlock: Have 4 cruisers on game board
- Ability: Force opponent to give PN after winning space combat
- Why: Steal promissory notes after combat victories.

**13. Federation of Sol (Claire Gibson)**
- Unlock: Control planets with 12+ resources
- Ability: Place 1 infantry when defending ground combat
- Why: Defensive infantry.

**14. Jol-Nar (Ta Zern)**
- Unlock: Own 8 technologies
- Ability: Reroll unit ability dice
- Why: Reroll unit abilities (BOMBARDMENT, AFB, SPACE CANNON).

**15. Argent Flight (Trrakan Aun Zulok)**
- Unlock: Have 6 units with AFB, SPACE CANNON, or BOMBARDMENT
- Ability: Roll 1 additional die for unit abilities
- Why: Extra die for unit abilities.

**16. Yssaril Tribes (So Ata)**
- Unlock: Have 7 action cards
- Ability: Look at action cards/PNs/secrets after activating system with your units
- Why: Information gathering.

**17. Ral Nel Consortium (Watchful OJZ)**
- Unlock: Be last player to pass during action phase
- Ability: When you declare retreat, immediately retreat up to 2 ships to adjacent system without other players' ships. Place command token from reinforcements into that system.
- Why: Enhanced retreat mechanics. TE faction.

**18. Ghosts of Creuss (Sai Seravus)**
- Unlock: Have units in 3 systems with alpha/beta wormholes
- Ability: Place fighters after moving through wormholes
- Why: Wormhole-dependent mobility.

**19. Clan of Saar (Rowl Sarring)**
- Unlock: Have 3 space docks on game board
- Ability: Place fighters/infantry at any space dock when producing
- Why: Production flexibility across multiple docks.

**20. Sardakk N'orr (G'hom Sek'kus)**
- Unlock: Control 5 planets in non-home systems
- Ability: Commit 1 ground force from adjacent planets during invasion
- Why: Invasion flexibility.

**21. Obsidian (Aroz Hollow)**
- Unlock: Have units in The Fracture
- Ability: Apply +1 combat in The Fracture
- Why: Late game Styx plays. TE faction.

**22. Yin Brotherhood (Brother Omar)**
- Unlock: Use one of your faction abilities
- Ability: Green prereq + skip prereqs when researching others' tech
- Why: Tech path flexibility.

**23. Last Bastion (Nip and Tuck)**
- Unlock: There are 3 galvanized units on game board
- Ability: Action cards cannot be canceled by Sabotage. Nekro cannot place assimilator tokens on your components.
- Why: Sabotage immunity and Nekro protection. TE faction.

**24. Xxcha Kingdom (Elder Qanoj)**
- Unlock: Control planets with 12+ influence
- Ability: Each planet exhausted to cast votes provides 1 additional vote
- Why: +1 vote per planet. Strong for influence-heavy factions.

**25. Council Keleres (Suffi An)**
- Unlock: Spend 1 TG after playing action card with component action
- Ability: Perform additional action after component action
- Why: Very situational.

**26. Arborec (Dirzuga Rophal)**
- Unlock: Have 12 ground forces on planets you control
- Ability: Produce 1 unit when another player activates system with your PRODUCTION units
- Why: Reactive production ability.

**27. L1Z1X Mindnet (2RAM)**
- Unlock: Have 4 dreadnoughts on game board
- Ability: Ignore Planetary Shield for BOMBARDMENT
- Why: Strong for BOMBARDMENT-heavy factions. Bypass defensive structures.

**28. Hacan (Gila the Silvertongue)**
- Unlock: Have 10 trade goods
- Ability: Spend TGs for 2 votes each
- Why: Convert TG wealth into political power.

**29. Naalu Collective (M'aban)**
- Unlock: Have ground forces in/adjacent to MR
- Ability: Look at neighbors' PNs and top/bottom agenda card
- Why: Information gathering.

**30. Firmament (Captain Aroz)**
- Unlock: Have a plot card in play
- Ability: Treat planets in systems with ships as controlled for scoring secrets
- Why: Fake control for secret objectives. TE faction.

**31. Mahact Gene-Sorcerers (No Alliance Commander)**
- Note: Mahact has no alliance commander ability.
